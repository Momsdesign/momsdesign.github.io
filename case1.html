Immuna

The simple way to book a time for vaccination

Since the arrival of the vaccinations for COVID-19, people in all target groups
have had trouble with booking a time for their vaccination. Whether it being by
calling to your health center or booking by web, the information for booking
regarding where, when and for whom has been lacking and by decentralizing
the method for booking we saw chaos, discomfort and crashed websites.
